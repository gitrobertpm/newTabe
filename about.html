ABOUT

A few words about this unique product from the inventor himself

"It's a powerful DIY tool which will educate the community on marijuana edible products.

The idea is to help enlighten a user on the dosing factors that come into play when consuming products containing cannabis.

By engaging the customer and explaining how the potency of a product can be influenced by the ingredients and the way they are prepared may really go a long way towards helping ease anxieties for some people.

My product is aimed at an entry level audience, so I really want to try to drop a lesson to hopefully help individuals avoid a bad experience."

TABE founder
