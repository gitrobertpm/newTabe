DIRECTIONS


ITEMS REQUIRED:
Egg, Bowl, Spoon, Oven


  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Warm coconut oil packet with warm water to a liquid state
  3. Mix dry ingredients with 1 egg & oil packet in a bowl
  4. Use non-stick cooking spray inside aluminum pan (rinse pan first)
  5. Pour mixed contents into pan
  6. Bake at 350° uncovered for 30 - 33 mins (check with toothpick)
  7. Allow to cool
  8. Enjoy

TIP: add a half cup of chocolate chips or a swirl of caramel to add some pizazz

ADVICE



To ensure a quality and pleasant experience, it is recommended that you start off with a small dose, wait 30 min, and gradually work up from there.


Paranoia and anxiety are among the leading causes of a bad experience.

So if the experience becomes too intense, remember to stay calm, remind yourself that it will pass, and just try to enjoy the journey.
